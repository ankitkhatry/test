About NSS

National School Of Sciences

NIST National School of Sciences, founded in 1993, is a leading educational institution in Kathmandu offering Science and Management programs. With plans to expand into Law, Arts, and Humanities, it aims to provide a comprehensive education from Pre-school to Grade 12. Founded by scientists and academicians, NIST emphasizes academic excellence, innovation, and moral values, nurturing over 1,000 students annually for success in both education and life.
